Key Features & Benefits

Apps we are building are future proof and ready to grow. These are benefits of working with us and our technology.

Interactive Development

During your time as a client, we will make sure our work is visible to you whenever it is possible. You will be able to track not only tasks being completed, but app being developed.

Mobile Ready

Your app will work well both as a responsive web-app, but also on mobile devices. IOS and Android features are supported, like notifications, local storage, offline work, camera, etc.

Deep Linking

One of highly sought after features of mobile apps is ability to "deep-link" or connect users to specific parts of the app from promotional emails or other places. We support this feature.

User & Data Management

Data of your users and other related data will not be locked but available to you through management panel, potentially through API as well, depending on your needs.

Analytics & Tracking

We will integrate your app with analytics and tracking tools of your choice, so you can track your users and their behavior. Usually these are some behaviour tracking like Google Analytics and performance tracking like LogRocket or Sentry.
